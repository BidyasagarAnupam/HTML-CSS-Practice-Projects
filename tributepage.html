Background

Pascal Siakim was born in Cameroon. He had been training to be a Catholic priest, but stumbled upon the basketball camp of former NBA player Luc Mbah a Moute in 2011.

Mbah a Moute took Siakim under his wing and Siakim moved to the US at the age of 16 and began attending God's Academy in Lewisville. He was recruited to play at New Mexico State University in 2013. In his college career, he won WAC Freshmen of the Year and WAC Player of the Year honors, before declaring for the NBA draft after 2 years.

Siakim was drafted 27th overall by the Toronto Raptors in 2016 and has gone on to win an NBA championship in 2019.
