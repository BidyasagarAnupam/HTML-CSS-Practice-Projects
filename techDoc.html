How to Order Chipotle

Technical document outlining how to properly order an item from Chipotle

Pick an Item

First, you must choose which menu item you want to order. The options are as follows:

  • Burrito
  • Bowl
  • Salad
  • Tacos

If getting the best value for your dollar is your priority, picking a bowl will allow you to maximize the amount of food you can get for your money.

Pick your Rice

Next you'll have to make a choice on the type of rice you want.

Chipotle offers cilantro lime white rice and cilantro lime brown rice. You can select either, ask for a double portion free of charge, or you can forego rice entirely.

Pick your Beans

Next, you must make a choice on what beans you'd like. Your options are either black beans or pinto beans.

Similar to rice, you can ask for extra beans free of charge. That being said, you could get both beans.

Pick your Protein

You'll have a variety of proteins to choose from. The selection is as follows:

  • Chicken
  • Steak
  • Carnitas
  • Barbacoa
  • Sofritas
  • Veggie

Keep in mind that if you opt for a veggie item, you'll be able to get guac free of charge!
